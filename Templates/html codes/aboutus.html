Who We Are

Everything we do at Burton started in the mountains. From getting the most out of every journey to chasing snow around the globe, we've charged ahead to innovate and change the way people enjoy the outdoors since day one.

The Team

The Burton Team's work embodies the Burton life. Yes, they're world champions, innovators and leaders in the sport, but they're also the perfect ambassadors for all things Burton. Providing crucial feedback on all our products while traveling the globe, they're our experts at making the most out of every day.
